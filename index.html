Welcome to our FOOD NUTRITION GUIDE!

Automatically count calories by taking food photos! We believe calorie counting should be as simple, fun, and easy as taking a picture of your food. Food Nutrition Guide can tell you the calories in your food just by analyzing your food photos.

How it works:
1) Upload a photo of your desired food,
2) Food Nutrition Guide recognizes your food,
3) It returns nutrition value for it.
